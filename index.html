BitTorrent Detection in Network Traffic (Thesis)
Python

Goal of my Bachelor's thesis summarized concepts of capturing of network traffic and finding best methods for analysis of captured packets in order to find BitTorrent Protocol traffic. My detection was working with unsecured and also with encrypted traffic. It detected unsecured connection basically with 100% accuracy. Secured connection analysis was a little bit less accurate as expected. The output of my script were IP addresses with Ports in XML format that were convinced or suspected of participating in BitTorrent traffic.
